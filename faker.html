    Career

    Faker is a well-known professional League of Legends player from South Korea. He was previously known on the Korean server as “GoJeonPa.” SK Telecom picked him up in the year 2013. Faker is currently playing mid-lane for SK Telecom T1 in the League of Legends Champions Korea (LCK).

    He was born in Seoul, South Korea, on May 7, 1996. Lee Sang-hook is his real name. Similarly, he is well-known for his high mechanical skill and an extremely versatile champion pool. He’d previously appeared in the films LeBlanc, Zed, Syndra, Azir, Ahri, and Ryze. He became the first player in the LCK to reach 1,000 kills. Following that, the second player to reach 500 games.

    He is one of only two players in League of Legends history to win the World Championship three times, in 2013, 2015, and 2016. Faker also won the 2014 All-Star Paris competition. Following that, in 2016, he won the Mid-Season Invitational tournament. In 2017, he won the Mid-Season Invitational tournament for the second time. Similarly, in 2016, he won the IEM World Championship. He has won a total of $1,254,240.23 in prize money throughout his career. In terms of prize money won across all eSports, he is currently ranked #64. He is widely regarded as the greatest League of Legends player ever. As a result of his talent, he owns three different League of Legends skins as a result of winning the World Championship: SKT T1 Zed in 2013, SKT T1 Ryze in 2015, and SKT T1 Syndra in 2016.
