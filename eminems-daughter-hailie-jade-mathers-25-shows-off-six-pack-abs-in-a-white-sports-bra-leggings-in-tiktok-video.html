     Hailie tried on different outfits as she stunned her TikTok followers. 

    Eminem’s daughter mentioned she was putting on the “loungewear” for the upcoming Fall season. 

    She also wrote that her wardrobe felt overall “cozy.”

    While changing into each clothing, the 25-year-old influencer always had her white slippers on. 

    With a wool robe on, Hallie showed off her incredible figure and cleavage while rocking a white sports bra.

    She kept the white leggings on after putting on a sleeveless turtleneck tank top. 

    At the end of the clip, the TikToker wore a navy blue bodysuit with matching leggings.  

    The Bazzi song, I Like That, played during the 16-second clip. 

    ON A BOAT

    Back in August, Eminem’s daughter delighted her Instagram followers with a couple of sexy bikini shots. 

    She appeared to be relaxing on the deck of a boat while out on the waters. 

    Hailie stunned in a busty neon bikini top and posed in a bright multicolor briefs for the snaps. 

    She let her brunette locks loose while donning stylish shades.

    The caption for the photos said: “Aye aye, captain.”

    HER FAMILY

    Eminem, also known as Marshall Mathers, shares Hailie with his ex-wife Kimberly Scott. 

    Four years after his daughter was born, the couple decided to tie the knot, but they divorced shortly after in 2001.

    The Slim Shady rap star and Kimberly gave it another shot and remarried in January of 2006, but filed for divorce in April that same year.

    Eminem adopted two other daughters, Whitney and Alaina, both of whom are related to Kimberly and Hailie, after they previously suffered a poor home life.

    PROUD FATHER

    Back in 2020, Eminem spoke publicly in March about his child and gushed about how “proud” he was of her.

    On Mike Tyson’s Hot Boxin podcast, the 8-Mile actor said: “Hailie is 23, no babies, she has a boyfriend, but she’s doing good.

    “She’s made me proud for sure, she’s graduated from college.”

    HAILIE AND EVAN

    Hailie has been dating Evan McClintock since 2016 after the two had met during high school.

    They both headed to Michigan State University where the influencer studied psychology and her beau studied economics, which led him to a career as an enterprise growth executive for Scout.

    Back in 2018, a source told the Daily Mail that Eminem has been “very approving” of Evan.

    The insider claimed: “Hailie’s family have welcomed Evan, they are a fantastic young couple who love cozy nights in rather than hard partying.”
