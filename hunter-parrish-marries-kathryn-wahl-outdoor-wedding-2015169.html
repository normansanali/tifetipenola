    Hunter Parrish Marries Kathryn Wahl in Stunning Outdoor Wedding

    Wedding in the weeds! Actor Hunter Parrish tied the knot with his longtime love Kathryn Wahl on Sunday, Sept. 13, in a beautiful outdoor wedding at the Flying Caballos Ranch in San Luis Obispo, Calif., his rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

    22 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Wedding in the weeds! Actor Hunter Parrish tied the knot with his longtime love Kathryn Wahl on Sunday, Sept. 13, in a beautiful outdoor wedding at the Flying Caballos Ranch in San Luis Obispo, Calif., his rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

    The bride wore a strapless white tulle gown with her long hair down in a flower crown. Parrish, 28, wore a brown vest over a casual white shirt. Their guests dined under the stars on wooden tables decorated with plants and flowers. 

    Many of the wedding’s attendees used the Instagram hashtag “#loversinparrishdise.” 

    "It was an intimate outdoor gathering surrounded by their friends and family,” a source tells Us. “They took off for their honeymoon after their wedding.”

    Wahl and Parrish met on the set of Weeds in 2010, when the blonde stunner served as a personal assistant while filming the hit Showtime series. They got engaged in December 2014 after several years together. 

    “I didn’t say no,” Wahl captioned an Instagram post at the time, flashing her diamond sparkler. 

    Wahl also posted a shot on Tuesday, Sept. 15, from her tropical honeymoon spot, with the caption, “this hashtag is really living up to its name. #loversinparrishdise.”
