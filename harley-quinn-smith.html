    Early Life and Childhood

    Harley Quinn Smith came to this world on 26th June 1999, in Red Bank, New Jersey, USA. She is currently 23 years old and her birth sign is Cancer. She was born to actress and television personality, Jennifer Schwalbach Smith and director, producer, and screenwriter Kevin Smith. Moreover, she was named after the Batman character “Harley Quinn.”

    Smith is the granddaughter of actress Grace Smith and Donald E. Smith and niece of Virginia Smith and Donald Smith, Jr. She is close friends with ‘Yoga Hosers’ co-star Lily-Rose Depp. They first met each other in kindergarten at the Hollywood Schoolhouse.

    Smith is a Disney lover. She frequents Disneyland often in her home state of California. Furthermore, she is a vegan and considers veganism an important part of her life as she loves animals.     Career and Professional Life

    Harley Quinn Smith made her acting debut in 2001, appearing in her father’s movie ‘Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back. In this film, she portrayed the child role of Silent Bob. Three years later, she did an uncredited cameo in the drama flick ‘Jersey Girl’ in which she appeared as Tracy Colelli. Then in 2006, Smith acted in the comedy movie named ‘Clerks II’. After this, she played a small role as Colleen McKenzie in the movie ‘Tusk’ which was written and directed by her father.

    Around this time, she also appeared in an episode of the TV show ‘Comic Book Men’. In 2016, she did the film ‘Holidays’. The same year, she reprised the role of Colleen McKenzie in the sequel of Tusk, ‘Yoga Hosers’. The following year, she made a guest appearance as Izzy Williams in the television series ‘Supergirl’. She also lent her voice to the character of Jodie Fischer in the series ‘Big Hero 6: The Series.

    In January 2016, it was announced that Smith will play her father’s character’s daughter in the half-hour comedy ‘Hollywood’. Similarly, Smith was cast as Lindsay in ‘All These Small Moments. It premiered at the 2018 Tribeca Film Festival in 2017.

    Career after 2017

    She joined John Barrowman and other co-hosts in the debut September 15, 2018 episode of DC Daily. Therefore, it was a discussion show focusing on DC Universe comic topics. And as of December 30, 2018, she continues to work for DC Daily.

    In early 2019, Smith rejoined her father’s View Askewniverse, playing Millennium “Milly” Falken in Jay and Silent Bob Reboot. According to Kevin Smith, Milly is part of a girl gang that is not unlike the girl gang of Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back. However, their goals, and motivations are different, and they were put together as a commentary on Hollywood’s use of youth and diversity in reboots and sequels.

    Kevin Smith says that Milly’s role in the gang is similar to Shannon Elizabeth’s character Justice, her character is the daughter of Jay and Justice. The movie was released later in the fall. She also had a small role in Quentin Tarantino’s Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, portraying a member of the Manson Family.
