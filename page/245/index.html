31 Jul 2024, 00:00

Answer Taking Down the Glass Shelves 1 Take both the fruit and vegetable drawers out of the refrigerator. 2 Locate the tabs on both sides of the drawer divider/water filter housing and press them in. 3 Pull the shelf away from the water filter housing by pressing in on the tabs on either side. 4 Remove the shelf by pushing it slightly up as you pull it out of the wall.
