    Jujutsu Kaisen Manga Chapter 239 Raw Scan and Spoilers

    In the latest chapter titled “BAKA(STUPID) SURVIVOR” of the manga, the story takes a new perspective, following a girl with rolled hair who’s running away from someone. Gege Akutami, the mangaka, surprises us by diverting from the expected plotline of Yuji’s return to action.

    The girl is furious at a character named Kenjaku for the events in the Culling Game. She questions the cruelty of giving life to cursed individuals only to take it away again. But her confrontation with Kenjaku takes a dark turn as she gets trapped by a jellyfish-like curse.

    Kenjaku reveals his lack of emotions towards the people involved and describes them as mere “seasoning and water” to him. He discloses a method of tracking reincarnated individuals and awakened sorcerers, leading to an ominous revelation.

    Hazenoki appears to confront Kenjaku, and their exchange is laced with tension and humor. Kenjaku hints at his plan to merge Tengen with the people of Japan. The chapter ends with Takaba’s unexpected arrival and survival against Kenjaku’s attack.

    The unexpected twist in Takaba’s resilience hints at a thrilling showdown in the upcoming chapter, leaving readers eagerly awaiting the next installment of the story.

    Where to read jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 239?

    Jujutsu Kaisen manga Chapter 239 can be found on the official Viz and Manga Plus websites. Readers can access the three most recent chapters of the series for free on both sites. However, to read earlier chapters, a subscription must be purchased. Moreover, Chapter 238 will also be available on the Shonen Jump+ mobile app, which requires a paid subscription for readers to access the entire series.

    Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 238 Summary

    In the latest chapter, Jujutsu Kaisen fans were treated to a long-awaited revelation – the true form of Sukuna, the King of Curses. This revelation has left fans excited and eager to learn more about the unfolding events in the series, particularly in Chapter 239.

    The previous chapter unveiled that Sukuna was able to return to his true form due to his unique ability to repair his flesh, distinct from his Reverse Cursed Technique. This became necessary after his intense battle with Gojo, which left him with a missing hand and considerable damage. In his weakened state, Sukuna couldn’t effectively use his Reverse Cursed Technique, prompting him to resort to his true form.

    To achieve this form, Sukuna utilized Megumi as a base, resuming Transformation as a curse-taking form. This hints at Sukuna possibly becoming a “full-blown” curse or a hybrid. The chapter provided a satisfying exposition of his true form, revealing details like his hand signs with various hands, including one holding Kamatoke, and confirming the presence of a mouth on his stomach.

    The chapter also featured a blurry but intriguing flashback in Feudal Japanese style, portraying Sukuna’s past battles. Additionally, we witnessed more of Kamatoke’s abilities and learned about Sukuna’s feats, such as wiping out the Sun Moon and the Stars Squad, the Five Void Generals, and repelling the darkness Pacification Force.
