    Career

    Dakota Fanning started her career as an actress and worked in several small plays with Towne Lake Arts Center in Woodstock, Georgia. After that, in the year 1999, she made her appearance in a brand commercial for a popular laundry detergent brand named Tide. She made her acting debut with her appearance in the popular television series titled ER in the year 2000.

    After that, she has played several small recurring roles in popular television series like CSI Crime Scene Investigation, The Practice, Spin City, Ally McBeal, and The Ellen Show. In the year 2001, she made her acting debut with her appearance in the movie titled Father Xmas and then played an important role in the movie titled I Am Sam along with Sean Penn.

    In the year 2002, she played the lead role of Allison Allie in the popular science fiction movie titled Taken. In the same year, she has worked in three other films titled Trapped, Sweet Home Alabama, and Hansel and Gretel. In the very next year, she has made her appearance in the movies like Uptown Girls and The Cat in the Hat. She has also worked as a voice-over artist in animated series like My Neighbor Totoro, Family Guy, and Justice League Unlimited.

    In the year 2004, she has made her appearance in a movie titled Man on Fire as Pita. In the same year, she has also played an important role in the tenth season of the popular television series Friends.

    In the year 2005, she has made her appearance in the American horror film titled Hide and Seek and also voiced the role of Lilo Pelekai in the video film titled Lilo & Stitch 2: Stitch Has a Glitch.
