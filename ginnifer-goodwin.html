    Name:Ginnifer Goodwin

    Hometown:Memphis, TN

    Birthday:May 21, 1978

    About

    A Midwest girl born in 1978, Ginnifer Goodwin rose to fame with roles on NBC series’ Ed and Big Love.

    She has landed roles in hit films including Something Borrowed (2011), Ramona and Beezus (2010), A Single Man (2009), Birds of America (2008), Walk the Line (2005), Win a Date with Ted Hamilton! (2004) and Mona Lisa Smile (2003).

    The actress split from beau Chris Klein in 2008; she was briefly engaged to actor Joey Kern in 2010.

    Goodwin also starred in the 2009 romantic comedy, He’s Just Not That Into You alongside A-List stars Jennifer Aniston,Bradley Cooper, Drew Barrymore, Jennifer Connelly, Scarlett Johansson and Justin Long.

    Most recently, Goodwin has been portraying Mary Margaret/Snow White on ABC’s fairytale drama, Once Upon a Time.

    In the fall of 2011, Goodwin began dating Josh Dallas, her costar on ABC’s Once Upon a Time. They are married and have two sons, Oliver born in 2014, Hugo born in 2016.
