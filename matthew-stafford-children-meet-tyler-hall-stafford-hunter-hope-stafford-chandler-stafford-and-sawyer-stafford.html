    Mathew Stafford and his wife Kelly have over the years become a loving couple.

    The two met when they were both students at Georgia. Matthew was the most talented quarterback on the team.

    Kelly was a cheerleader for the Georgia Bulldogs. They were engaged long before Matthew Stafford was selected as the team’s franchise quarterback with the first overall choice in the 2009 draft.

    They married in 2015 and decided to start a family right away. They have four children together.

    Meet Tyler Hall Stafford, Hunter Hope Stafford, Chandler Stafford, And Sawyer Stafford

    Mathew and Kelly have four children together: 3-year-old twins Chandler and Sawyer , 1-year-old Hunter, and their fourth daughter named Tyler Hall Stafford..

    Kelly made her fourth pregnancy announcement in March. She revealed their new child is a girl, according to a post on Instagram.

    Tyler was born in the NICU at St. Joseph Mercy Oakland on June 26 at 9:11 a.m.

    Tyler is “another sweet young girl to complete our lady squad,” according to Kelly.

    They were with their baby daughter for ten hours before doctors opted to keep her in the NICU because “her lungs required some care, even though she was full term.”
