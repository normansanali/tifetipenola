    Chelsea Green has plans to return to the WWE in the near future. Following her WWE release, Green reinvented herself elsewhere and found her footing. She has now deactivated her OnlyFans page in preparation for her rumored return to the ring.

    It appears that WWE has a rule regarding having a fan site page. Chelsea Green, who previously had a page on OnlyFans, no longer does. When you click the link to her page, a “Sorry, This Page Is Not Available” notification appears to let you know the page has been taken down.

    Chelsea Green developed her skills in a number of professional wrestling organizations after being let go by WWE, including Impact Wrestling and GCW. The former WWE Superstar routinely uploads provocative photographs to her OnlyFans page. However, fans won’t be able to view her on the site any longer.

    Ringside News exclusively reported that Chelsea Green is on her way back to WWE. The only question was figuring out the right time for her debut.

    Xia Li was recently identified as the Smackdown mystery attacker. Chelsea, however, was the subject of widespread speculation before she was identified. Now, Fightful Select reported Green’s return to the company is imminent.

    Chelsea Green made several appearances on NXT before getting called up to the main roster for a cup of coffee before her injury and eventual release. Even before being a part of the Tough Enough series years prior, Green was involved in a mistress angle with Brie Bella and Daniel Bryan. She would sign a contract with WWE in 2018 and stick around until 2021.

    Chelsea Green’s recent departure from IMPACT and the closure of her OnlyFans page may now feed rumors of a WWE comeback. Naturally, this will also fuel rumors that Green’s husband, Matt Cardona, will make a comeback. We’ll attentively monitor any current discussions on Chelsea Green.
