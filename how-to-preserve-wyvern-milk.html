    How often can you get milk from a Wyvern?

    When a wyvern (and any dino really) is a baby, the food stat cannot reach full capacity. In general, it takes a little over an hour for a wyvern to go through 300 food. Therefor if you‘re baby is born with 900 food, it will start off needing milk at least every 3hours roughly.

    Do you force feed Wyvern milk?

    Milk when it’s a baby is much more important than milk when it’s closer to full grown. Milk gives 1200 food, but depending on the level the baby’s max won’t be near that for a while. You may have to force feed because they sometimes bug out until their food is over 1200.

    How long does it take for a baby Wyvern to starve?

    Wyverns lose 1 good point per 10 seconds, and 360 points per hour. So 1 wyvern milk will sustain a wyvern for 3.33 hours due to it’s 1200 food points. If it’s a low level, it might not have 1200 points and have to be force fed milk to avoid starvation.
