    May 31 — Temple Square Concert Series: The BYU-IDAHO Faculty Quartet of Edward Ashton, violin; Monte Belknap, violin; Kevin Call, viola; and Robert Tueller, cello, will perform Beethoven's String Quartet in B-flat major, Op. 18, No. 6, and Brahms' String Quartet in A minor, Op. 51, No. 2 in the Assembly Hall at 7:30 p.m. Free admission.

    June 6 — Temple Square Concert Series; Concerts in the Park: The Utah National Guard 23rd Army Band will start off the summer concert series at 8 p.m. at the Brigham Young Historic Park, southeast corner of State Street and 2nd Avenue. Free parking, (enter at 59 East North Temple) and free admission. For more information call 240-3323.

    June 7 — Temple Square Concert Series: Italian pianist Massimiliano Frani will perform Mozart's Fantasy in C minor K 475, Mendelssohn's Variations Serieus Op. 54, and Chopin's Sonata in B-flat minor Op. 35, in the Assembly Hall on Temple Square. Free admission.

    Through July 12 — BYU Museum of Art Exhibit, BYU Campus: "Walk in Beauty: Hozho and Navajo Basketry," a display of 38 Navajo baskets. Mondays, Thursdays and Fridays, 10 a.m.-9 p.m., and Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Saturdays, 10 a.m.-6 p.m. Free admission.
