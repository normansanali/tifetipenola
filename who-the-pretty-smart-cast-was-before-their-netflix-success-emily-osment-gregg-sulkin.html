    Who The 'Pretty Smart' Cast Was Before Their Netflix Success

    Netflix is certainly crushing the game with its recent releases of hit series, including one of the most-watched shows ever, Squid Game. While the gore might be a bit much for some viewers, the streaming platform just dropped their newest feel-good sitcom, Pretty Smart.

    12 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Netflix is certainly crushing the game with its recent releases of hit series, including one of the most-watched shows ever, Squid Game. While the gore might be a bit much for some viewers, the streaming platform just dropped their newest feel-good sitcom, Pretty Smart.

    The series follows the life of Chelsea, played by none other than Emily Osment, who found herself heartbroken and moving in with her charismatic sister and her three roommates in sunny Los Angeles. The show, spanning 10 episodes, has certainly become a hit amongst fans, and we certainly don't blame 'em.

    Related: The Truth About Emily Osment And Miley Cyrus' Relationship

    Emily Osment plays alongside love interest, Gregg Sulkin, who much like Osment, made his debut on Disney. Considering viewers have recognized a few familiar faces on the hit series, who is the rest of the cast? And what have they appeared in prior to Pretty Smart?

    7 Emily Osment

    Emily Osment is certainly the biggest name attached to the hit Netflix series, considering the actress has been in the game for quite some time now. Emily gained worldwide recognition playing the role of Lily (and Lola) in the popular Disney series, Hannah Montana, alongside Miley Cyrus, Billy Ray Cyrus, and Jason Earles.

    Osment is also the younger sister to Sixth Sense actor, Haley Joel Osment. Prior to playing Chelsea in Pretty Smart, Emily has also appeared in Spy Kids 2: The Island Of Lost Drama, and Spy Kids 3D: Game Over. In addition to acting, Emily has also dabbled with singing throughout her career, having her music be featured on an array of Disney shows.

    6 Olivia Macklin

    Olivia Macklin plays the role of Claire in Pretty Smart, and while her acting is obviously up to par, the Netflix series marks one of Olivia's very first major acting gigs.

    Olivia had secured a number of small roles prior to playing Emily Osment's on-screen younger sis, some of which include recurring roles in Filthy Rich and La To Vegas. Olivia also appeared in the period film, Radium Girls, however, after her time with Netflix, we're certain we'll be seeing her pop up a lot more now.

    5 Michael Hsu Rosen

    Michael Hsu Rosen portrays the hilarious on-screen influencer, Jayden, in Pretty Smart. Prior to the series, Michael appeared in a handful of TV and film projects including his recurring role in Tiny Pretty Things, and his guest appearance on The Good Doctor.

    Related: This Is What Fans Really Think About Netflix's Season 3 Of 'You'

    As if the acting weren't enough, Rosen is also a man of many talents, having a background in professional dance and singing. According to Elle, Michael wished to pursue the arts so much that he dropped out of Yale "in order to pursue the stage," and while it was a risky move, it certainly paid off.

    4 Gregg Sulkin

    Much like his co-star, Emily Osment, Gregg Sulkin also got his big break on Disney. Sulkin appeared alongside Brenda Song on Pass The Plate before securing a full-time role on the third and fourth season of Wizards of Waverly Place, where Gregg played Mason Greyback, Selena Gomez' on-screen love interest.

    Gregg later moved on to Pretty Little Liars and Runaways before landing his role on Pretty Smart, where he plays none other than fitness trainer and gym owner, Grant. While Gregg is British in real life, the actor has done a pretty stellar job at putting on an American accent for the series, leading many fans to believe he was actually from the United States to begin with. Talk about good acting!

    3 Cinthya Carmona

    Cinthya Carmona has gained prominence since appearing in Pretty Smart playing the role of Solana. The former lawyer turn spiritual guide became a fan-favorite character, proving herself to be quite the actress.

    Related: 'Don't Look Up': Everything The Cast Has Said About The Netflix Show

    Prior to her time with Netflix, Carmona appeared in a handful of television projects, including East Los High and Greenhouse Academy, but it's clear Pretty Smart stands out as her biggest role to date. However, we know it's only the beggining for Cinthya from here on out.

    2 Geoff Ross

    Geoff Ross plays the role of Howard in Pretty Smart, Claire's boss, and comedic relief at the local caf√©. While he began acting back in 2008, Ross didn't fully pursure the art until a decade later.

    In 2018, he landed a small role in a CollegeHumor Originals all while snagging a recurring role in Anime Crimes Division. After landing spots on Reno 911!, Pet Friendly, and of course, Netflix'sYou, which is currenlty airing its third season, Geoff made his way over to his biggest role yet in Pretty Smart.

    1 Kevin Miles

    Kevin Miles may not be a permanent character on the Netflix series, however, he's still a standout star. Kevin portrayed the role of David, who appears in three episodes throughout the series.

    While many fans will recognize him from Pretty Smart, he's often recognized for playing the role of none other than Jake from State Farm! Yes, you read that right, Kevin Miles took on the role of Jake from State Farm back in 2017. The actor has also appeared in countless other projects including Underdogs, Emanuel And Me, and Miles Away, to name a few.
