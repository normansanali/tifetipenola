    Who is Carly Rae Jepsen?

    The Hollywood music industry has seen a lot of talents both males and females. And today we are going to talk about the 33-year-old, Carly Rae Jepsen. She is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actress best known for her single "Call Me Maybe". Afterward, she signed to a joint worldwide record deal with Schoolboy Records and Interscope Records.

    Carly has received multiple accolades, including three Juno Awards, a Billboard Music Award, and an Allan Slaight Awards. In 2015, she sold over 25 million records worldwide.

    Carly Rae Jepsen was born on November 21, 1985, in Mission, British Columbia, Canada. As of 2019, she is 33 years old and her birth sign is Scorpio. Her parents Alexandra and Larry Jepsen raised her alongside two children. She has an older brother, Colin, and a younger sister, Katie and belongs to Danish, English, and Scottish descent.

    Jepsen considered a career in music instruction from an early age as her parents and stepparents were all teachers. She attended Heritage Park Secondary School, where she pursued her early passion for musical theatre.

    Jepsen auditioned for Canadian Idol where she performed her original song and finished in third place. She released her debut album, Tug of War in September 2008. Her breakthrough came with the lead single, "Call Me Maybe" which was the best-selling single of 2012 worldwide. Also, the single topped the charts in 18 countries.

    Jepsen's new album, Dedicated came out in May 2019 and to date she is active in theatre, films, and music. She has appeared in a few television shows such as Shake It Up, Castle, Comedy Bang! Bang!, etc.

    The couple initially were best friends but shortly took their relationship into next level. Jepsen and James first met at a songwriting camp in Nicaragua in the US. The pair has collaborated for Jepsen's album Dedicated as well.
