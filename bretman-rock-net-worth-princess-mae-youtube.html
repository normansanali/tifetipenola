    When you are a beauty guru, looking good is part of the job description. Makeup truly is an art, and just like painting, it requires tools. Bretman Rock and Princess Mae give their fans a healthy dose of glamour every day. But, have you ever wonder how much money they make?

    Beauty Influencer of the Year

    Bretman Rock became E! People’s Choice Beauty Influencer of the Year in 2019. Walking alongside the hottest celebrities, musicians, and personalities, Bretman owned the red carpet. He effortlessly slayed the red carpet, serving looks left and right. Shocked over the win, Bretman took to Instagram to thank everyone who voted for him.

    The Philippine-born beauty influencer based in Hawaii reached popularity several years ago for his sense of humor, beauty tips, and the sibling dynamic he shares with his sister Princess Mae.

    In 2017, and at just 19 years old, Bretman was recognized as one of the 30 most influential teens by Time Magazine. But the glamour of the Hollywood carpet did not overshadow what was really on Bretman’s mind and in his heart.

    Bretman and Princess’s Father Passed Away

    Before the People’s Choice Awards, Bretman lost his father, who passed away in the Philippines. The news was confirmed by Bretman’s sister, who posted a heartfelt tribute on her Instagram for their dad.

    Bretman shared he almost back up attending but decided to go to the PCAs to celebrate his dad and the love and acceptance he always received. The charismatic YouTuber told Balitang America, “my dad really understood me at such a young age and really supported me literally before I could even walk, and he told me that he always knew that I was gay. I remember when I was turning four, they got me this big truck, and inside were barbies.”

    With the night’s big win, there was no doubt who Bretman wanted to dedicate his most significant achievement to: “I would really like to dedicate it to the parents out there who love and support their children unconditionally,” he added.

    Bretman’s Net Worth

    The media personality has over 15 million followers on Instagram and almost 8 million subscribers on YouTube. Bretman has an estimated net worth of 1.3 million dollars. The beauty guru also makes extra income from sponsored Instagram posts. Additionally, he recently joined forces with Wet n Wild to launch its wildest collection to date: Jungle Rock. This is the first time that the makeup line collaborates with an influencer.

    Princess Mae

    She first became popular on YouTube and Vine thanks to collaborations with her brother Bretman Rock. They made many pretty popular videos together.

    Princess is a single teen mom who inspires many young girls. She often includes her daughter in the videos she publishes with Bretman. After Cleo was born, she found herself a new boyfriend named Chris. They created a new YouTube channel together, which is called LovingUs Family.

    LovingUs Family YouTube Channel

    Chris suddenly was very active on social media platforms. He even was on Bretman’s channel a couple of times, although everyone didn’t like him. Many people thought that Chris was a “clout chaser” and used Bretman and Princess for popularity.

    At one point, Princess confessed via DM that she had to cut ties with her YouTube family channel with Chris completely. They had their first baby together; his name is Ezékiel. There has been a lot of rumors about their break up saying he cheated on her.

    Princess then confirmed their breakup. She wrote on her Instagram, “I definitely did everything that I could to keep my family together and kept everyone else happy. I felt the need to let him go now that I realized it’s not going to stop. I pray for him…”

    Savage X Fenty Ambassador

    Princess Mae’s LovingUs Family channel has 1.3 million subscribers. The 20 years old influencer has 2.5 million followers on her Instagram. She has collaborated with popular brands like Rihanna’s lingerie line Savage X Fenty. Like her brother, she makes extra income from sponsored posts mainly of makeup brands. Princess has a net worth of 1 million dollars.

    Bretman Rock is one of the most unproblematic Youtubers. He seems to care about his art, family, and fans genuinely. When people talk about him is to spread positivity and happiness by sharing his funny videos. Some polemic has surrounded Princess Mae, but she remains strong despite becoming a mom at such a young age and losing her father.

    The siblings are now closer than ever. After losing such an essential member of their family, they know that they can always count on each other. Thanks to their popularity, it’s a relief that they can make a living by themselves and support their family financially. These stars’ careers keep growing, and fans are excited to see more of them in the future.
