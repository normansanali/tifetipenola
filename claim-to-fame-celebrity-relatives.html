    1. Maxwell

    Clues: Maxwell lied about his grandfather’s fame as a singer while revealing that his known relative is his grandfather.

    celebrity Relative: After being sent home for using his phone in the first episode, Maxwell is identified as being the grandson of star Chuck Norris.

    Status on the program: Week 1, Elimination.

    Celebrity relative: When he is sent home in the second episode, it is revealed that his cousin is Zendaya.

    Clues: Louise told her other castmates that her famous relative is her sister, who is an athlete, but she tried to throw them off by claiming that her greatest accomplishment was winning the Honda-Broderick Cup, an honor for college-level female athletes.

    Celebrity relatives: Louise, whose full name is Adria Louise Biles, disclosed in the first episode that she is Simone Biles’ younger sister.

    4. Amara

    Clues: Amara hinted that her grandmother, an actress, is her most known relative, but she lied about her most prestigious prize being an AVN, which refers to Adult Video News.

    Relatives: Amara disclosed in episode 1 that she is the granddaughter of the actress Whoopi Goldberg.

    Show Status: The contestant is still in the tournament.

    Clues: Brittany said that her famous relative is her father, an athlete who won the Super Bowl and was elected into the Hall of Fame.

    Celebrity relative:  Brittany confirmed in episode 2 that she is the daughter of former Green Bay Packers quarterback Brett Favre.

    Clues: Dominique lied about her famed relative becoming an actor, thus her famous relative may be a musician or an athlete.

    7. L.C.

    Clues: L.C. is not only lying concerning her name but also about her father being a known relative. She did explain, though, that her famed relative is an Emmy-winning actor.

    Celebrity relative: Unknown, although one of the most popular fan theories is that she is related to Keke Palmer.

    Status: The contestant is still in the tournament.

    Celebrity relative: Although he initially told the cast that his famed relative is his father, he eventually disclosed that his famous relative is, in fact, his twin. Unknown, but fans are certain he is Laverne Cox’s twin brother.

    9. Logan

    Clues: Although Logan told the cast that his famed relative was his grandfather, it is in fact his cousin, an ACM-winning artist. Additionally, Logan disclosed that he is from Georgia and composes country music.

    Celebrity relative: Unknown; however, a few of his clues indicate that his cousin could be either Luke Bryan or Jason Aldean.

    Status: The contestant is still in the tournament.

    10. Pepper

    Clues: The only thing we do know about Pepper’s prominent relative is that she lied about them being comedians at her introduction.

    Celebrity relative: Fans’ top guesses for the celebrity relative’s identity range from Bette Midler to Dean Martin.
