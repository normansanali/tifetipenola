    Former professional American cyclist Lance Armstrong married his long-time girlfriend and mother of his children, Anna Hansen, in a beautiful ceremony in France.

    Anna and Lance have been in a relationship for over 14 years and finally decided to tie the knot and be legally bound to each other. Armstrong shared the happy news of their wedding on social media with pictures from the wedding with his wife and kids.

    Anna Hansen: Newly Married Wife Of Lance Armstrong

    Anna Hansen is the long-time girlfriend and the newly married wife of the former professional cyclist Lance Armstrong.

    She is an American philanthropist and a yoga teacher who came into the limelight after being involved romantically with the star cyclist.

    Anna was born in Fort Collins, Colorado, to parents David and Sharon on May 24, 1981. Hansen graduated with a bachelor’s degree in biology from the University of Colorado and got her first job through the pre-med honor society right after college.

    Hansen is very outgoing and a philanthropist who loves being a part of charity events, which is why she became a board member at Camp Wapiyapi, where children with cancer and their siblings enjoy summer camp activities.

    Anna has also worked as a program director for a non-profit program called First Descents in Colorado, where she met her husband, Lance.

    Currently, she works as a yoga teacher after receiving her training license in Austin, TX, and has her Yoga Studio named MOVE with Anna with a youtube channel and in-house services.

    What Is Anna Hansen And Lance Armstrong Net Worth?

    Anna Hansen’s estimated net worth is around $1.5 million from her career as a yoga teacher from her yoga studio, and her Youtube channel.

    However, her husband, Lance Armstrong’s estimated net worth is around $50 million from his career as a professional cyclist.

    The former American road racing cyclist was worth more than $50 million in the past, but his series of controversies and scandals lost him a lot of endorsements.

    Lance Armstrong And Anna Hansen’s Wedding Photos

    Lance Armstrong and his newlywed wife, Anna Hansen, posted a series of wedding photos on their respective Instagram accounts whereLance shared his happiness with the world.

    He wrote, “Best. Day. Ever. I married the love of my life, @annahansenarmstrong. So special having our kids there as well. It was an evening full of laughter and plenty of joyful tears.”

    The couple has been in a relationship for over 14 years and has two children, Olivia Marie and Max Armstrong, who were both present at the wedding.

    Armstrong further expressed how grateful he is for his wife, saying,

    “Anna, you have been my absolute rock the past 14 years, and let me be clear, I would not have survived them without you. I am so proud of the couple we have become – It took us doing the work, the tough work, and I am so glad we did. I love you immensely and will always be here for you and our family.”

    Where Did Anna Hansen And Lance Armstrong Get Married?

    Anna Hansen and Lance Armstrong married in France at Chateau La Coste, a sculpture park and an art destination.

    The ceremony was beautiful and memorable, with all their children celebrating their special day as Lance’s children from his former marriage also attended the wedding as the bridesmaid and the best man.

    They held a small wedding ceremony with their family and celebrated their marriage with wine from the organic winery at Chateau La Coste.
