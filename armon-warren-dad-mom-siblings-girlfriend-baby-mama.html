    Reginae Carter is one of the four children of American rapper/singer Lil Wayne. She is his first child and was born to the Hip-Hop star and his high school sweetheart Toya Johnson on 29 November 1998. Lately though in addition to being kenned because of her famous father, Reginae is also known for playing out her relationship with Ar’mon Warren on a WeTV show called Toya and Reginae. This show which kicked off on 24 August 2023 follows Reginae and her mom Toya as they let cameras in on their real lives. Because we get to see the relationship between Reginae and Ar’mon on the show, we bring to you a tell-all writing about Ar’mon. Here’s everything you need to know about him.

    Ar’mon Warren Girlfriend: Is He Still Dating Reginae Carter?

    Episode 1 Toya and Reginae of the show documented Reginae and Ar’mon together as they hit some issues in their relationship, having just had planned a move together. Nevertheless, they were together around this time. Fast forward to today though, they were no longer a couple. Speaking on Fox 5 on 24 August 2023, Reginae explained that she is now officially “single”. While also admitting “Ar’mon is hot,” she confirmed that they are no longer together. She also assured her well-wishers that she was doing well. Earlier, in May, she detailed in a live video why she broke up with him. She explained why she felt “live-bombed” by her ex-boyfriend. Seemingly hurt, she also discussed how he did not check on her when her home was burglarized. Instead, according to her, he was busy making a YouTube video. Around this time, Ar’mon too was throwing jabs at each other. Their breakup was clearly a messier one.

    At the time of this writing, Reginae and Ar’mon no longer followed one another on social media. 24-year-old Reginae, at this point, continued doing what she usually does as an actress, rapper, singer, and social media star. As of now, she also was a member of the group “OMG Girlz” alongside Zonnique Pullins (Tiny Harris’ daughter).

    And then just in April 2023, these two exes also had shut down speculations about domestic violence. Reginae literally dispelled in a TikTok Live that it was Ar’mon but herself responsible for her hand injury.

    Until actually December 2023 the estranged couple was seemingly manifesting marriage in the future. Around this time, Ar’mon also was recorded addressing DJ Akademiks calling his girlfriend at the time “a b**ch.”

    They both also occasionally documented their romance on social media. They showed people as they exchanged gifts on Christmas Eve. In all those years they dated, they celebrated their anniversary on December 25th. One time, during their heartfelt gift exchange moment, Ar’mon expressed his love for his girlfriend by giving her a beautiful diamond promise ring.

    Who Is Ar’mon Warren’s Baby Mama?

    Ar’mon Warren has a daughter named Legacy Dior Warren. She was born on 5 April 2020 to Warren’s baby mama, Esmeralda Adriana, a content creator and businesswoman.

    Like Ar’mon, 28-year-old Esmeralda also is a social media star. She can be found on IG @mamii_es with 58 posts and 547K followers as of August 2023. Here, she introduces herself as a “woman of God.”

    Before, Ar’mon also had girlfriends with the names Amari Allure and Ar’mon Warren. She enjoyed as many as 158K followers on her IG @amariallure_. She is a social media influencer and YouTuber.

    Ar’mon Warren Dad

    Ar’mon Warren’s dad is no more. He most likely passed away in April 2023. On a YouTube video at this time around, he gushed about his regrets that he should not have held a grudge and should have let him meet his daughter. This was his biological dad.

    Ar’mon also has one more dad, the one who raised him. He is Brian Brown and he can be found on IG @mrbmbrecords with 60.1K followers. Brian seemingly is the owner of BMB Records, an independent label specializing in music projects.

    Ar’mon Warren Mom

    Ar’mon Warren’s mother is Shiquita Lanier of Atlanta, Georgia. She turned 43 years old in August 2023.

    Often on social media Ar’mon shows glimpses of him treating his mom like a queen and also surprising her with beautiful, expensive gifts.

    How Many Siblings Does Ar’mon Warren Have?

    Ar’mon is one of the six siblings. It is not yet possible for us to know all about them, but here are a few things we learned. One of them is Dwight Burton, a basketball player. Also, there is Trey Traylor, an American singer, musician, and social media star. He turned 25 November 2022. Ar’mon and his brother Trey perform together and run their highly popular music YouTube channel. Also, these two have the same mother and different fathers. They have five sisters who are also their mother’s offspring: Shimea “Mea” Ellis (born 1996), Shaniah Traylor (born 1999), Erin Hathaway (born 2012), Erianna Hathaway (born 2016), and Love (born 2020).

    Ar’mon’s half-siblings on his father’s side are Keelan Paul, Tyler Stewart, Miya, Dwight Burton, “Day Day” Burton, K. Burton (from the “KB AND KARLA” YouTube channel), and other sisters and brothers.

    Ar’mon’s siblings Trey and Shaniah share a father. They have other half-siblings and/or cousins which include Billy, Kam, Jaden, Nakiyah, Iyanna, Zoey, Aaliyah, Riley, Chase, and Ryder.
