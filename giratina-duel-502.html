    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Giratina (Japanese: ギラティナ Giratina) is a figure in Pokémon Duel. It was made available in version 6.2.5.

    Abilities

    Distort ディストーション
    At the start of your turn, instead of an MP move, this Pokémon may move through an adjacent Pokémon to a point 1–2 steps away from that Pokémon. When it does, this Pokémon may change its form. If it changes its form, until the end of your next turn, any effects of its battle opponent's Ability that would increase damage decrease that damage instead. Your turn ends.

    Trivia

    Origin

    This figure features the Origin Forme of Giratina. Draco Meteor and Shadow Sneak are both moves in the Pokémon games that Giratina can learn, the first one via Move Tutor. Never-Ending Nightmare and Devastating Drake are Z-Moves in the games, although Giratina cannot use them in its Origin Forme.
