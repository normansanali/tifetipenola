    Whoops! Vicki Gunvalson gave her Instagram followers more than a Throwback Thursday on Thursday, Dec. 11. The Real Housewives of Orange County star accidentally shard a nude image of herself while attempting to post a picture of Beats headphones.

    The since-deleted snap was a picture of a black Beats headphone's package, and in the reflection of the plastic wrap, was Gunvalson's nude breasts and stomach.

    After realizing her mistake and deleting the pic, the Bravo vet apologized to fans on Facebook and explained the snafu.
