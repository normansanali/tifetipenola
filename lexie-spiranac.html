    Paige Spiranac, a former US golfer, is a well-known figure on social media. She has more Instagram followers than golfing greats like Rory McIlroy and Tiger Woods combined, with a remarkable 3.6 million.

    Despite having ended her professional golf career, she had a successful amateur career. Her family is active, and her older sister Lexie Spiranac is also from an active household. As an athlete, Lexie was also awarded a college athletic scholarship. She participated in track and field events for Stanford. She reportedly competed on the team that won the 4A State Championship in 2004 and the 5A High Jump State Championship in 2007. Lexie cleared 1.63 metres to record her personal best at the collegiate level in the high jump division.

    While both Spiranac sisters were born and raised in Monument, Colorado, Paige was born in Wheat Ridge, Colorado, and Lexie in Chicago, Illinois. It appears that Lexie is single despite the lack of any information about her personal life.
