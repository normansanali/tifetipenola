    Update, 4/7: Anne Hathaway is a mom! The actress welcomed her first child, a son, with husband Adam Shulman, Hathaway's rep confirmed to E! News. The baby, named Jonathan Rosebanks Shulman, was born on March 24 in LA.

    "The baby is extremely healthy and is surrounded by friends and family in LA," a source told E! "Anne and Adam are ecstatic to be parents."

    Congratulations to the new family! 

    Update, 1/4/16: Anne Hathaway confirmed her pregnancy on Instagram last night, sharing a photo of herself posing on a beach and explaining why she decided to post the image: "I noticed I was being photographed. I figure if this kind of photo is going to be out in the world it should at least be an image that makes me happy (and be one that was taken with my consent. And with a filter :)" See the image and caption in full below.
