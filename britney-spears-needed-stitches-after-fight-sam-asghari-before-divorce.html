    Highlights

    • Britney Spears allegedly required medical attention after a physical fight with Sam Asghari, according to TMZ's investigative special.
    • Sam Asghari reportedly believes Britney cheated on him with an employee and was physically aggressive towards him multiple times.

    TMZ’s new investigative special Britney Spears: Divorce & Despair has made a series of shocking allegations amid Britney Spears and Sam Asghari’s ongoing divorce, including that the pop star required medical attention after a fight between the pair turned physical.

    In the special, released on August 24, TMZ founder Harvey Levin alleged, “She and Sam were going at it in a hotel room, and it got so bad [that] she tripped and hit the coffee table, cracking her head open. She needed stitches.”

    So far, neither Britney nor Sam have directly commented on the shocking allegations. However, other reports suggest the claims made in TMZ’s program are untrue. For example, Us Weekly claims that its sources deny Britney cracked her head open during a fight with Sam.

    The entire TMZ program suggests that Britney and Sam had a chaotic relationship. Executive producer Charles Latibeaudiere claims Sam eventually grew tired of Britney’s “volatility,” saying it became “too much” for him, leading him to file for divorce.

    Britney Allegedly Attacked And Cheated On Sam

    The latest allegations come after it was reported that Sam’s decision to divorce Britney following 14 months of marriage was motivated by his belief she was cheating with an employee.

    Insiders tell TMZ that Sam believes Britney asked an employee to film her while naked and that she’s hooked up with at least one of her male staff members. A source also claims there’s a video of Britney in a “compromising position” with an employee.

    RELATED: Sam Asghari’s Reaction Revealed After Britney Spears Dances With Shirtless Men During “Divorce Party”

    Moreover, it’s been claimed that Britney has been physically aggressive with Sam multiple times in the past. Earlier this month, sources claimed to TMZ that Sam “frequently complained” about fights with Britney turning physical, adding security has had to break up arguments between the pair various times.

    Moreover, the same report claim there was a time when Britney attacked Sam while he was sleeping. Sources tell TMZ the singer “flew off the handle and began punching him.” The publication adds that Sam “did not strike back but was stunned as his wife pummeled him.”

    TMZ points out that this alleged incident happened around the time Sam was seen in public with a black eye and bite marks earlier this year.

    Sam Allegedly Wants A Big Divorce Payment

    Sam and Britney signed a prenuptial agreement last year prior to their wedding, but there have been various allegations that Sam is planning to challenge it, as it only entitles him to $1 million following 2 years of marriage, to be capped at $15 million following a decade together.

    He’s apparently requesting spousal support, while one report says he’s also trying to negotiate a bigger settlement from Britney in exchange for not releasing personal information to the public.

    “[Sam is] attempting to negotiate concessions beyond his prenup and threatening to go public with extraordinarily embarrassing information about Britney unless he gets paid,” an insider claimed to Page Six. “So that’s what Sam is focused on,.

    Nonetheless, Sam has denied the claims he’s interested in getting more of Britney’s money.

    “There are many claims that Sam is challenging the prenup and threatening to exploit his ex-wife with videos,” his rep said. “However, all these claims are false, as no negative intention has ever been directed towards her and never will be. Sam has always and will always support her.”

    Britney and Sam’s divorce remains ongoing.
